Betting on football is a recommended option for beginners in the world of betting.

Initially, football betting was a form of entertainment. For example, pari-mutuel can be mentioned — a long-standing pool system that originated in Britain at the beginning of the 20th century with the following conditions:

  • Outside the stadium, fans bought tickets at a minimal price (later tickets began to be distributed through newspapers, where they would cut them out);
  • On the cards, fans entered the results of upcoming matches of "game number one", which they believed were most likely;
  • Coupons were handed to a pool collector agent or sent by mail to the organizing company.

A specialized office guaranteed that there would be at least one winner during the week. The lucky ones were determined according to the number of points earned among all fans who sent in their cards.

The pari-mutuel system made it possible to earn big money by placing the first football bets. For example, Michael Elliott, risking only 2 pounds sterling, earned more than 3 million pounds. He managed to accurately predict 8 matches that ended in a 2:2 draw.

The pool system was successful until "betting shops" began to appear in the form in which they exist today. Fans of "game number one" got the opportunity to place bets with much greater comfort.
